Oxy the OpenPNGStudio mascot
==========

Oxy was compiled during the early builds of OpenPNGStudio and decided to stick
around, known behavior includes occasional headbutts.

His purpose is simple: to serve as an example model for users experimenting with
the program's features.

Beyond being a sample model, Oxy can also be used for streaming purposes, just
make sure to comply with his LICENSE.txt.
